## Data Attribution

The data presented within this application is aggregated from publicly available government sources:
- **Statistics Canada (Stats Can)**: Quantitative trade volume and macro export data are sourced from Statistics Canada and are utilized in accordance with the Open Government Licence - Canada.

The raw data itself remains the property of the respective Canadian government entities and is utilized within this application purely for informational, analytical, and public visualization purposes.
